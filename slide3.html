Modern Celebrations

Today, Beltane is celebrated in cities like Edinburgh with a vibrant festival of fire, music, and performances. People wear elaborate costumes, dance around the Maypole, and enjoy theatrical shows.

The festival attracts visitors from around the world, keeping the ancient traditions alive while adding a modern, artistic touch.

It is a night full of spectacle, energy, and community spirit, showing how historical festivals can be adapted and celebrated in the 21st century.
