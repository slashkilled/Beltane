Rituals & Celebrations

Beltane is celebrated with large bonfires, music, and dancing. People often jump over the fires as a way to bring good luck and protection.

Traditionally, rituals included decorating maypoles with ribbons, dancing around them, and offering blessings to the land and animals.

Flowers and greenery were used to symbolize fertility and growth. Modern celebrations still feature fire, dancing, costumes, and performances.
